How Auto Lyrics Works

After choosing a title and selecting your desired artist, the lyric generator takes action by coming up with song lyrics in the same style as the chosen artist. Auto Lyrics accomplishes this by utilizing Markov chains.

What Is A Markov Chain?

In short, a Markov chain is a stochastic process that gives us the power to predict the future, based on past and previous states. You can learn more about Markov chains here.
